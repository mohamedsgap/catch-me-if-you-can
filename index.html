Zombie vs Human

Welcome to Zombie vs Human

GAME RULES:

  • Every single player can move up to 3 steps horizontally or vertically.
  • Each player start the game with 100% health and initial weapon.
  • During the game to collect weapons to become stonger, so try to collect strong weapon.
  • When the two players are close to each other the fight start.
  • The winner is the last man standing.

Enjoy the game :)
